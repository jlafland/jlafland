Popular topics . . .


Illuminate Your Inner Light


An introduction to the new field of Energy Medicine. Learn to see, hear, and feel your energy field and tune in to your home frequency. Explore the cause and effect between your thoughts, your energy field and creating wellness and joy in your life.


Coping with Cancer and Illness


The How, The Why, and the Why Me. Sharing, learning, and finding growth behind illness. Boosting and cleansing the energy field to promote wellness and eliminate treatment toxins. Group discussion, visioning exercises, relaxation techniques, coping with fear.

Find the Hidden Answers


Intuition and clairvoyance are that, “tug on your sleeve, a pull on your fish line.” Learn exercises and skills to bring out your own in-born capabilities. Your only limitation is your own imagination!

Workshops/Playshops

Workshops are hands-on, custom-tailored to meet your group's goals, and are always light, fun, and entertaining. Past workshops include cancer retreats, motivational and networking groups, and substance abuse facilities.
